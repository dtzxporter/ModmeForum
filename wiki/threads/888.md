<p>Thread By: Lethal Peelz<br /><p style="text-align:left;">So i have been making a custom mysterybox model for my csgo map and after alot of testing i finally found a method that works.</p><p style="text-align:left;"></p><p style="text-align:left;">The first thing to do is setting up the model. This depends if your mystery box has unique animations but for this tutorial im just gonna replace the model. What i did for this box is i binded tag_origin and tag_animate with the mesh group and binded j_hinge to the hinge.</p><p style="text-align:left;"><img style="max-width: 500px;" src="http://i.imgur.com/4IloSLn.png"></p><p style="text-align:left;"></p><p style="text-align:left;">After that&#39;s done, you just export the model and then export2bin or use kronos. then you put the xmodel in model_export and set it up in APE.</p><p style="text-align:left;"></p><p style="text-align:left;">IMPORTANT: Make sure to set the model type to animated, otherwise it wont play the animations</p><p style="text-align:left;"><img style="max-width: 500px;" src="http://i.imgur.com/psVb8KX.png"></p><p style="text-align:left;"></p><p style="text-align:left;">FORGET THIS</p><p style="text-align:left;"><span style="color:#ff0000;">[Next thing to do is editing the zbarrier for the mystery box. Search for zbarrier in the GDT tab. In that gdt you will find a zbarrier called &quot;zmcore_mysterybox&quot;. First thing to do is to duplicate the zbarrier and call it &quot;zmcore_mysterybox_copy&quot;. This will be used to set the original box back to normal if you dont want your box anymore.</span></p><p style="text-align:left;"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><img style="max-width: 500px;" src="http://i.imgur.com/aYCtUuX.png"></span></p><p style="text-align:left;"><span style="color:#ff0000;">Now the last thing to do is to edit the original zbarrier. Just replace the models and anims and for this im just going to change &quot;p7_zm_der_magic_box&quot; to my model. If you want to you can also change the animations like if you were porting the origins mystery box.</span></p><p style="text-align:left;"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><img style="max-width: 500px;" src="http://i.imgur.com/6O0Y70f.png"></span></p><p style="text-align:left;"><span style="color:#ff0000;"></span></p><p style="text-align:left;"><span style="color:#ff0000;">Now save, compile and enjoy;)</span></p><p style="text-align:left;"><span style="color:#ff0000;"></span></p><p style="text-align:left;"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><img style="max-width: 500px;" src="http://i.imgur.com/iQwqdBy.jpg"></span></p><p style="text-align:left;"><span style="color:#ff0000;"></span></p><p style="text-align:left;"></p><p style="text-align:left;"><span style="color:#ff0000;">] </span></p><p style="text-align:left;">BIG EDIT:</p><p style="text-align:left;">I messed up.
